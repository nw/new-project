Occurring three times a year, MCA PRIME TIME is an after-hours series that taps into the creative pulse of Chicago by providing a platform for artists and key cultural groups in the city. An electrifying social experience, the series transforms the museum through an eclectic mix of live music, performance art, film screenings, and interactive programs.

Partnering with music tastemakers Pitchfork for the upcoming event, PRIME TIME offers new ways of being refreshed through a variety of live performances and art activations.

PRIME TIME is sponsored in part by Anne Kaplan and Perrier.
